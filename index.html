Nemo Data Views Prototype

These prototypes were created as a space to experiment with approaches to displaying data to teachers and students. By creating rough prototypes in code, we're able to stress-test data visualisation ideas and flows under different scenarios.

The functionality reflects real stories from the backlog, using plausible products and student scenarios. However, the design is placeholder only and doesn't represent Nemo's finished look!
